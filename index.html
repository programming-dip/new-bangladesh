Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Harnessing the power of technology and creativity to drive groundbreaking advancements in education, healthcare, and industry, paving the way for a prosperous future.

Sustainability

Building an eco-conscious nation by prioritizing renewable energy, reducing waste, and preserving natural resources for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Developing world-class transportation, digital connectivity, and urban planning to enable seamless mobility and economic growth across the country.

Empowerment

Fostering an inclusive society by promoting education, skill development, and equal opportunities to uplift communities and unlock potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Poverty is not created by the poor people. It is created by the system we have built, the institutions we have designed, and the concepts we have formulated. Poverty is not an accident. It is not the fault of the individual. It is a systemic problem, and it persists because we design our financial, economic, and social systems in ways that exclude and marginalize millions of people. If we redesign these systems with human dignity at their core, we can eradicate poverty and create a fairer, more inclusive world for everyone."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
